The goal of the game:
-is to NOT put it in the hole

How to play:
- Game starts by placing powerups. Each player will have a powerup in the beginning. Each player only has 30 seconds to place powerup
- Placement Controls: WASD to place, QE to rotate

- Once every player has placed powerup, putting begins.
- Putting Controls - Any key to begin sequence, and then Space to Hit ball.

Current bugs:
- Can place powerups above hole
